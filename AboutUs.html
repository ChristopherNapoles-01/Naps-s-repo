About Us

CoVid-19 Safety Tips is a website created to serve as guide for people who want to learn some information about Corona Virus Disease 2019. The site provides different tips and information that will help people fight the current pandemic. The website originated from the idea that the creator can do something to help people fight the virus during this pandemic. The content on this website is free for every one who wants to be informed about CoVid-19 and some tips that can help them in protecting theirselves from the deadly virus.
